DIRECTOR & EDITOR

Omar Sherief is a Director & Editor based in Cairo, Egypt. Studies Film Editing at the High Cinema Institute.

In 2018, he launched his YouTube channel teaching filmmaking and photography which has since gained over half a million views and around fifty thousand subscribers. In 2020, Omar co-founded his own production house "Cinehunters" that since made over 7 commercial ads for various brands including Infinix mobile, Lejour, and AutoVroom.

Preferring cinema over football, Omar spent his childhood in front of the big screen where he believed that one day he could add his fingerprint. He believes that "A director is a jack of everything, master of none." and that's why he can manage any aspect of the production including Editing, Cinematography, and Sound Design.

Three things that Omar loves the most: Suits, Dogs, and Chocolates.
